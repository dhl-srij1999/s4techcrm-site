About S4Tech CRM

S4Tech CRM is designed to give small teams enterprise-grade CRM functionality without the complexity. Built with NestJS, PostgreSQL, and Docker, it focuses on clarity, extensibility, and security.

Mission

Empower teams to close more business with a modern, reliable CRM.

Stack

Node.js (NestJS), TypeORM, PostgreSQL, Docker, and modern frontend tooling.
